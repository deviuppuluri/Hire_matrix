Job Role: DevOps Engineer

Job Description: We are looking for a skilled DevOps Engineer to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will have a strong understanding of development, operations, and cloud technologies. As a DevOps Engineer, you will work closely with developers, QA engineers, and IT professionals to streamline the software development lifecycle (SDLC) and automate processes to enhance product delivery and system reliability.

  • Automation & CI/CD Pipelines: Design, implement, and manage automated CI/CD pipelines to ensure smooth code integration and deployment.
  • Infrastructure Management: Ensure the scalability, availability, and security of infrastructure by monitoring system performance and handling system resource provisioning.
  • Monitoring & Troubleshooting: Troubleshoot production issues, working closely with development teams to identify root causes and implement fixes.
  • Security: Implement best practices for security in the deployment pipeline and ensure systems are compliant with security standards.

Required Skills & Qualifications:

  • Strong experience with CI/CD tools such as Jenkins, GitLab CI, CircleCI, or similar.
  • Proficiency in version control systems, particularly Git.
  • Hands-on experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud) and container orchestration tools like Kubernetes and Docker.
  • Experience with monitoring and logging tools like Prometheus, Grafana, ELK Stack, Datadog, or similar.
  • Strong understanding of Linux/Unix systems and networking protocols.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills, communication skills, and ability to work in a collaborative team environment.
