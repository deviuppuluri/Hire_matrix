Technical Support Specialist

Accenture

Job Role: Technical Support Specialist

Job Description: A technical support specialist's job is to help customers with technical issues, such as hardware and software problems, by providing support and assistance:

  • Troubleshooting: Diagnose and solve issues with hardware and software, including connection problems, slow performance, and data access issues.
  • Support: Provide technical support by phone, email, chat, or in person.
  • Remote access: Take control of a customer's computer remotely to resolve issues.
  • Communication: Communicate with customers and clients using professional written and interpersonal skills.
Technical support specialists may also be responsible for:
  • Maintaining a help desk tracking system
  • Working with network administration to troubleshoot network outages
  • Maintaining user accounts
  • Rebooting servers and routers
  • Testing and evaluating new technology
  • Conducting electrical safety checks on computer equipment

Eligibility: B.Tech ,B.Com More than 65%

Passout : 2021, 2022, 2023, 2024

Job Type: PART-TIME JOB

Salary Package:20,000 INR to 25,0000 INR per annum

Experience Required: 3 or 3+ years in software development, proficient in Java, Python, or similar languages.
