Job Role: Financial Accountant

Job Description:We are seeking an experienced and detail-oriented Financial Accountant to join our finance team. The ideal candidate will play a key role in preparing financial statements, managing accounts, and ensuring the accuracy and integrity of our financial records.

  • Financial Reporting: Assist in the preparation of annual budgets, forecasts, and variance analysis.
  • Accounts Payable and Receivable: Oversee accounts payable and accounts receivable processes to ensure timely payment and collection.
  • Taxation: Stay updated on changes in tax regulations and accounting standards, advising management on their impact.
  • Financial Analysis: Conduct financial analysis to identify trends, variances, and potential areas of improvement in company performance.

Required Skills & Qualifications:

  • Strong proficiency in accounting software (e.g., QuickBooks, Xero, SAP, Oracle).
  • Excellent understanding of Microsoft Excel for financial modeling and reporting.
  • In-depth knowledge of accounting principles, standards (IFRS, GAAP), and financial regulations.
  • Strong attention to detail and ability to work under pressure to meet deadlines.
  • Ability to maintain confidentiality and handle sensitive financial information with integrity.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to collaborate across departments.
