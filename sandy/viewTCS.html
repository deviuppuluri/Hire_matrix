Job Role: Machine Learning Engineer

Job Description:We are seeking a talented Machine Learning Engineer to join our innovative team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for developing and deploying machine learning models to improve our products and services. As a Machine Learning Engineer, you will work with large datasets, apply machine learning techniques, and implement solutions that drive business growth and enhance customer experiences.

  • Model Development and Deployment: Design, build, and deploy machine learning models to solve business problems across various domains.
  • Data Processing and Feature Engineering: Collaborate with data engineers and analysts to clean, preprocess, and analyze large datasets.
  • Model Evaluation and Optimization: Optimize models for performance, speed, and scalability.
  • Research and Innovation: Experiment with and apply new machine learning techniques, frameworks, and libraries to improve existing models or create innovative solutions.

Required Skills & Qualifications:

  • Proficiency in programming languages like Python, R, or Java.
  • Experience with machine learning libraries and frameworks such as TensorFlow, PyTorch, Keras, Scikit-learn, or similar.
  • Strong understanding of data structures and algorithms.
  • Knowledge of cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP) and deploying models at scale.
  • Experience in data preprocessing, feature engineering, and working with large datasets.
  • Ability to work with SQL and NoSQL databases to query and manipulate large datasets.
